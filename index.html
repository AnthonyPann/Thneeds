About

Everybody needs a Thneed! A Thneed is a highly versatile object knitted from the foliage of a Truffula Tree. According to the Once-ler, it is "A-fine-something-that-all-people need." It costs $3.98 (approximately $29.30 adjusted for inflation) and can be worn throughout the year.

Thneeds can take on a LARGE variety of forms for different purposes, such as:

  • Shirts, socks, hats
  • Pillows, sheets, curtains
  • Covers for bicycle seats
  • Toothbrush holders
  • Thneedle Thoup that can cure chest and back pain (how this is possible is unknown)
  • Carburated rust eliminator
  • Food (Tastes like bread, but without the crust)
  • Slingshot & windshield wiper
  • Vests, capes, robes, and other fancy garb
  • Tents, ponchos, and hoodies

Summer Fashion

Before you hit the beach this summer, make sure you have a Thneed! It's a fine thing that all people need. When you need to beat the heat, and need to cover your feet on the beach, consider purchasing a Thneed. When used as a bathing suit, the Thneed is sure to make a splash.
